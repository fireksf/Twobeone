We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your information:

**Encryption:**
- All data transmitted between your device and our servers is encrypted using HTTPS/SSL
- Passwords are hashed using industry-standard algorithms
- Database connections are encrypted

**Access Controls:**
- Strict access controls to limit who can view your data
- Partner-specific data isolation (only you and your partner can see your shared data)
- Role-based access for administrative functions

**Infrastructure Security:**
- Secure cloud hosting with Supabase
- Regular security updates and patches
- Automated backups for data recovery
- Monitoring for suspicious activity

**Authentication:**
- Secure authentication with Supabase Auth
- Optional two-factor authentication support
- Session management and automatic logout

### 4.2 Your Responsibilities
While we implement strong security measures, you also play a role:
- Keep your password confidential
- Log out of shared devices
- Report suspicious activity immediately
- Use a strong, unique password

### 4.3 Data Breach Notification
In the unlikely event of a data breach affecting your personal information, we will:
- Investigate the breach promptly
- Notify affected users within 72 hours
- Provide clear information about what data was affected
- Offer guidance on protective measures

## 5. Your Privacy Rights

### 5.1 Access and Portability
You have the right to:
- Access all personal information we hold about you
- Request a copy of your data in a portable format
- Review your journal entries, prayers, and responses

### 5.2 Correction and Update
You can:
- Update your profile information at any time
- Correct inaccurate information
- Modify your preferences and settings

### 5.3 Deletion
You have the right to:
- Delete specific content (journal entries, prayers, responses)
- Delete your entire account
- Request complete data deletion

**Account Deletion Process:**
1. Go to Settings → Account → Delete Account
2. Confirm deletion (this action is irreversible)
3. All your data will be permanently deleted within 30 days
4. Your partner will be notified of the disconnection

**What happens when you delete your account:**
- All your personal data is permanently deleted
- Your partner's data remains intact (their journal entries, prayers, etc.)
- Shared content created by your partner remains visible to them
- You will be disconnected from your partner
- Your partner can connect with a new partner using a new invite code

### 5.4 Data Retention
We retain your information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide services. When you delete your account, we delete your data within 30 days, except:
- Information required for legal compliance (up to 7 years)
- Aggregated, anonymized data used for analytics (cannot be linked back to you)

### 5.5 Opt-Out Rights
You can:
- Disable push notifications in your device settings
- Opt out of marketing emails (we don't send them currently)
- Disconnect from your partner at any time
- Pause or disable specific features

### 5.6 Export Your Data
You can request a complete export of your data:
- All journal entries
- All prayer requests
- All question responses
- All milestones
- Profile information
- Format: JSON or CSV
