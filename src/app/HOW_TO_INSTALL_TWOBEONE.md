## 📱 For Android Users (Chrome, Edge, Samsung Internet)

### **Automatic Install (Easiest)**

1. **Open TwoBeOne** in your mobile browser (Chrome recommended)
   - Visit: `https://your-app-url.com`

2. **Wait for the Install Banner**
   - After a few seconds, you'll see a banner at the bottom
   - The banner says "Install TwoBeOne"

3. **Tap "Install App"**
   - A popup will ask for confirmation
   - Tap "Install"

4. **Done!** 🎉
   - TwoBeOne is now on your home screen
   - Open it just like any other app
   - No need to open your browser anymore!

### **Manual Install**

If the banner doesn't appear:

1. **Open the Menu**
   - Tap the three dots (⋮) in the top-right corner

2. **Select "Add to Home Screen"** or **"Install App"**
   - Look for this option in the menu

3. **Tap "Add" or "Install"**
   - Confirm the installation

4. **Find TwoBeOne on your home screen**
   - You're all set!

## 🍎 For iPhone Users (Safari)

### **Installation Steps**

1. **Open Safari Browser**
   - TwoBeOne must be installed through Safari (not Chrome)
   - Visit: `https://your-app-url.com`

2. **Tap the Share Button**
   - Look for the share icon at the bottom middle of Safari
   - It looks like a box with an arrow pointing up (📤)

3. **Scroll Down and Find "Add to Home Screen"**
   - Scroll through the options
   - Look for the icon with a "+" symbol

4. **Tap "Add to Home Screen"**
   - You'll see a preview of the app icon
   - The name "TwoBeOne" will be pre-filled

5. **Tap "Add" in the Top Right**
   - Confirm the addition

6. **Done!** 🎉
   - TwoBeOne appears on your home screen
   - Tap to open it like any app

### **iPhone Tips:**
- ✅ Always use Safari (not Chrome) for installation
- ✅ The icon might take a few seconds to appear
- ✅ You can move the icon anywhere on your home screen
- ✅ To uninstall: Long-press the icon and select "Remove App"

## 💻 For Desktop Users (Windows, Mac, Linux)

### **Chrome or Edge**

1. **Visit TwoBeOne** in your browser
   - Go to: `https://your-app-url.com`

2. **Look for the Install Icon**
   - In the address bar, you'll see a small icon
   - It looks like a computer screen with a download arrow (⊕)

3. **Click "Install"**
   - A popup will ask to install TwoBeOne
   - Click "Install"

4. **Done!** 🎉
   - TwoBeOne opens in its own window
   - It appears in your applications/programs
   - Pin it to your taskbar for easy access

### **Alternative Method:**

1. **Open the Menu**
   - Click the three dots (⋮) in the top-right corner

2. **Select "Install TwoBeOne"**
   - Look under "More tools" or directly in the menu

3. **Click "Install"**
   - Confirm the installation

## ❓ Frequently Asked Questions

### **Q: Is this a real app?**
A: Yes! It's a Progressive Web App (PWA) - it works exactly like apps from the app store, but installs directly from the web.

### **Q: Will it use a lot of storage?**
A: No! It's much smaller than typical apps - usually 5-15 MB total.

### **Q: Do I need to update it manually?**
A: No! Updates happen automatically. You'll see a small notification when an update is available.

### **Q: Can I uninstall it?**
A: Yes! Uninstall it just like any other app:
- **Android:** Long-press icon → Uninstall
- **iPhone:** Long-press icon → Remove App
- **Desktop:** Right-click → Uninstall

### **Q: Will it work without internet?**
A: Yes! You can view previously loaded content offline. New content requires internet.

### **Q: Do I need to sign up again after installing?**
A: No! Your login is saved. Just install and open.

### **Q: Why don't I see it in the app store?**
A: TwoBeOne is a PWA, which means it doesn't need app store approval. This allows us to update faster and keep it completely free!

## 🆘 Troubleshooting

### **"I don't see the install option"**

**Android:**
- Make sure you're using Chrome, Edge, or Samsung Internet
- Wait at least 30 seconds on the page
- Visit the site at least twice
- Clear your browser cache and try again

**iPhone:**
- You MUST use Safari (other browsers don't support this)
- Make sure you're tapping the Share button (not the menu)
- Scroll down in the share menu to find "Add to Home Screen"

**Desktop:**
- Make sure you're using Chrome or Edge
- Check the address bar for the install icon
- Try accessing the browser menu (⋮)

### **"The icon looks wrong or blurry"**

- Uninstall the app
- Clear your browser cache
- Reinstall the app
- The icon should now appear correctly

### **"It won't install at all"**

Make sure:
- You have a stable internet connection
- Your browser is up to date
- You have enough storage space on your device
- You're visiting the correct URL

### **"I installed it but can't find it"**

**Android:**
- Swipe up to open app drawer
- Search for "TwoBeOne"
- Check the home screen

**iPhone:**
- Swipe right to search
- Type "TwoBeOne"
- It should appear in the results
