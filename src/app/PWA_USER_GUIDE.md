# 📱 TwoBeOne - How to Install on Your Device

## Welcome! 🎉

TwoBeOne can be installed on your phone, tablet, or computer just like a regular app. Here's how:

---

## 📱 iPhone & iPad (iOS)

### Step 1: Open Safari
- TwoBeOne must be opened in **Safari** (not Chrome or other browsers)
- Go to your TwoBeOne web app URL

### Step 2: Tap the Share Button
- Look for the **Share** button at the bottom of the screen
- It looks like a square with an arrow pointing up ⬆️

### Step 3: Add to Home Screen
- Scroll down in the Share menu
- Tap **"Add to Home Screen"**
- You can edit the name if you want
- Tap **"Add"** in the top right corner

### Step 4: Open the App
- Find the TwoBeOne icon on your home screen
- Tap it to open the app
- It will open full-screen like a native app! 🎊

### ✨ iOS Benefits:
- ✅ App icon on home screen
- ✅ Full-screen (no browser bars)
- ✅ Fast access
- ✅ Works offline
- ✅ Push notifications (iOS 16.4+)

---

## 🤖 Android Phone & Tablet

### Method 1: Install Prompt (Automatic)
1. Open TwoBeOne in Chrome or Samsung Internet
2. Wait a few seconds - you'll see an **install banner** at the top or bottom
3. Tap **"Install"** or **"Add to Home Screen"**
4. Confirm the installation
5. The app icon will appear in your app drawer!

### Method 2: Browser Menu (Manual)
1. Open TwoBeOne in Chrome
2. Tap the **three dots** (⋮) in the top right
3. Select **"Install app"** or **"Add to Home Screen"**
4. Confirm installation
5. Done! Open from your app drawer

### ✨ Android Benefits:
- ✅ Full app experience
- ✅ Appears in app drawer
- ✅ Fast loading
- ✅ Works offline
- ✅ Push notifications
- ✅ Background sync

---

## 💻 Desktop Computer (Windows, Mac, Linux)

### Chrome, Edge, or Opera:
1. Open TwoBeOne
2. Look for the **install icon** (⊕ or 🖥️) in the address bar (right side)
3. Click the icon
4. Click **"Install"**
5. The app opens in its own window!

### Alternative:
1. Click the **three dots** (⋮) in the top right
2. Click **"Install TwoBeOne..."**
3. Confirm installation

### ✨ Desktop Benefits:
- ✅ Own app window (not a browser tab)
- ✅ Add to taskbar/dock
- ✅ Launch from Start menu/Applications
- ✅ Keyboard shortcuts
- ✅ Faster than browser

---

## 🔔 Enable Push Notifications (Optional)

After installing, you can enable notifications:

### On iPhone/iPad:
1. Open TwoBeOne
2. Go to **Settings** (bottom right)
3. Tap **"App"** tab
4. Tap **"Enable Notifications"**
5. Allow notifications when prompted

### On Android:
1. Same as above, or...
2. System will prompt you automatically
3. Tap **"Allow"**

### On Desktop:
1. Browser will ask for notification permission
2. Click **"Allow"**
3. Or enable in Settings → App tab

Once installed, TwoBeOne works even without internet!

### What works offline:

## 🆘 Troubleshooting

### "I don't see the install button"
**iOS:** You must use Safari browser. Other browsers can't install apps.
**Android/Desktop:** Try refreshing the page. Some browsers need HTTPS.

### "The app icon is missing"
- Check your home screen or app drawer
- Try installing again
- On iPhone, check all home screen pages

### "Notifications aren't working"
- Go to Settings → App tab
- Check notification permission status
- Enable notifications for each type you want
- Check your device's notification settings

### "App won't open"
- Clear browser cache
- Uninstall and reinstall the app
- Make sure you have internet connection for first launch

### "Content not loading"
- Check your internet connection
- Try refreshing (pull down on screen)
- Check Settings → App → Clear Cache if needed
