## 💜 Brand Voice Guidelines

**Tone:** Warm, encouraging, faith-filled, authentic

**Do:**
- Use inclusive language ("you and your partner")
- Lead with Scripture and biblical principles
- Celebrate small wins and progress
- Be encouraging, never judgmental
- Use emojis sparingly and meaningfully (💜🙏📖)

**Don't:**
- Be preachy or condescending
- Use religious jargon that might confuse new believers
- Make assumptions about relationship stage
- Overpromise ("This will fix your marriage")
- Use manipulative marketing tactics

**Sample Posts:**
✅ "Growing together in faith takes intentionality. Start with just 10 minutes today. 💜"
❌ "Your marriage is failing without daily devotionals! Download now!"

✅ "What if you knew your partner as deeply as God knows them? TwoBeOne helps you get there."
❌ "Secrets your spouse is hiding! Discover them with our questions!"
