# 📱 Generate iOS Icons - Quick Start

## 🎯 You're 2 Minutes Away from Perfect iOS Icons!

Your TwoBeOne app has a beautiful icon ready (two hearts becoming one 💜), but iOS needs PNG files.

### ⚡ Generate All Icons Now:

1. **Open Your Browser** and navigate to:
   ```
   /public/generate-ios-icons.html
   ```

2. **Wait 10 Seconds**
   - Icons generate automatically on page load
   - You'll see all 10 sizes appear with previews

3. **Click "Download All Icons"**
   - All 10 PNG files download instantly

4. **Upload to `/public/icons/`**
   - Replace the placeholder files
   - Keep all 10 PNG files

5. **Test on iPhone**
   - Open Safari → Your app URL
   - Share → "Add to Home Screen"
   - Beautiful icon appears! ✨

---
